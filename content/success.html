Success Stories

Learn from companies and people who have had great successes in China's social media. Understand what it takes to be able to reach Chinese social media users.

Case Study: Lifa Air

The cleantech company Lifa Air has moved to the Chinese market in the beginning of 2015 and has had great use of social media in their marketing. Lifa Air has a Chinese partner who manages their social media activity in cooperation with a Lifa Air representative. Edifier Technology invested 21 million euros in Lifa Air and they are now together producing an end consumer air purifier. Since their first launch of the air purifier product on 12th of October 2015, they have been very successful in sales. Lifa Air’s Weibo page has over 20 000 followers and their WeChat page about 10 000 (May 2016).

According to Vesa Mäkipää, CEO and founder of Lifa Air, their social media campaign has been very important in their success on the Chinese market. The use of opinion influencers via WeChat has been especially effective. You can read more about the opinion influencers in the active marketing section.

In their campaigns, Lifa Air gives prizes that are drawn among users that comment on their posts. This motivates followers to be active on your page and increases visibility of your campaign.

When we asked advice on marketing in Chinese social media, Eppu Mäkipää from Lifa Air had the following to say: “In social media you can save in the costs, but you need to work hard to achieve and maintain results. Your followers will certainly include smart, educated and critical people, so you need to be honest and accurate when making statements, otherwise it will be a blowback to you.”

So, what can we learn from Lifa Air?

  • If possible, take advantage of your Chinese partners expertise in the Chinese market and social media environment.
  • Word-of-mouth advertisement is very effective. Use opinion influencers! You can read more about the opinion influencers in the active marketing section.
  • Use rewards in your campaigns!
  • Be honest and accurate!

Case study: Durex

Durex has used Weibo as a main marketing channel and succeeded in this greatly. But how did Durex succeed in this? Durex strategy envolves precise humorous marketing based on current events. For example Durex road to success started on flod in China. One man posted a picture where he had put Durex condoms in he's shoes to keep his foot dry. Durex made this official and "retweeted" it. This led to huge amount of attention in Weibo.

As another Durex example the company posted an interesting ad On Father's day.

Also Durex created a cute condom character called Little DuDu, which Durex used to interact with customers. Durex resbonded to questions with little DuDu's character and posted some sexual information in a cute way.

So, what should we learn about Durex Weibo marketing?

  1. Humour works in social media marketing in China
  2. Follow trends and post something that is somehow connected to trends.
  3. Interact with customers.
