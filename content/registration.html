Registration

Weibo is one of the most popular way to advertise your company. It is also the most easiest way to do it and if you are going to advertise your company this is the way to start. It can be used similarly as twitter with some own features. We will show you how can you use weibo so that everyone could do it.

However there are some constraints to succeed in marketing in Weibo.

  • You should make your posts in china.
  • You should make your posts as authentic as possible.
  • You should have chinese telephone number to register.

How to register a corporate weibo account?

Steps:

  1. Go to http://e.weibo.com/register/register, if not found google weibo registeration.
  2. Fill in the fields. Everything should be trivial. Nickname should be your Company's name or similar.
  3. After you have filled fields. Mobile verification pops up. You need to choose the right area code to receive verification. You will need Chinese mobile number to do this.
  4. You should receive activation code to your telephone and fill it in the field. After this pops up a window which wants your Nickname, Birthday Gender and Location. Everything else should be trivial except gender. The left one is male and the right one is female. Location should be set to other.
  5. Next you should choose your interests at least one is needed.
  6. After this you should go to your email,which you gave in the step 1. and find verification email and click the validation link. Then your registration should be done and you can start to customize your account and add posts.
