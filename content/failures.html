Failures

Don't make the same mistakes that some other major companies have made. It is important to know what shouldn't be done in social media.

Case study: Kalevala Jewelry

Kaleva Jewelry has a Weibo page which is not official. The page was made by somebody that Kalevala doesn't know. Fortunately the page is not active anymore. When it was active all the marketing posts were done with horrible translating. Most of the posts didn't make sense at all.This Weibo page still harms the Kalevala Jewelry's brand by making Kalevala seem unreliable company.

Case study: Finnair

Finnair service Weibo account provided close to 0 service. It did not manage to respond properly to customers. Also it posted wrong information about Finnair and Finland. Later the manager was replaced and the biggest problem solved, but still there is a lot of room for improvement.

Case study: Marimekko

Also Marimekko didn't have the best manager at first and the posts content was poor. Marimekko didn't have a lot of followers but after real-life activities and celebrity campaign it has improved. Also the content is better nowadays. Bad Weibo page had negative influence on Marimekko's brand.

Case study: Iittala

Iittala uses Weibo as a Instagram. It simple posts photos. There is no interaction with the customers. Iittala should use Weibo a lot more efficiently. For example big Chinese star is an Iittala fan and even used her own collection in a very popular TV series. However Iittala did not respond this in any way. Instead, some other medias have made articles about the tableware in the TV and introduced Iittala. Iittala could have made huge impact with that kind of marketing in China, but didn't

So, what should we learn about these Weibo marketing failures?

  1. It is important to have a good associates in social marketing.
  2. You should interact with your customers in your social media page.
  3. Use opportunities when they are offered.
