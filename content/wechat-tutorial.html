WeChat tutorial

WeChat is one of the most popular way to advertise your company. It is something between Facebook and Whatsapp.

However there are some constraints in marketing in WeChat.

  • You should make your posts in Chinese. You could make your posts in English, but this is not recomended
  • You should have official chinese id card when registering official account.
  • You should have chinese telephone number to register.
  • You should have chinese business license.
  • You should have chinese organization code to register.
  • You have to make an apllication letter to register.

How to register a official WeChat account?

Steps:

  1. Go to http://apply.wechat.com, if the link doesn't work Google WeChat official registeration.
  2. Fill in the fields. Everything should be trivial. E-mail adress should not be you personal e-mail, but companys general Company's name or similar.
  3. After you have filled fields and pressed next. The next page looks like this.
  4. Everything should be trivial. Until you reach Company intro.
  5. You should write a companys intro. Also you need to upload official company certificate, company profile, business card of contact person, etc.
  6. After this you need to write a proposal where you descripe what are you going to do with your account. You need to upload the proposal in a separete file also.
  7. After you have filled in all the fields you can press next.
  8. After this you need to fill the account details. Everything should be trivial. And when you have filled the fields then press next.
  9. After this you should go to your email, which you gave in the step 1. Find the verification email and click the validation link. Then your registration is done and you can start to customize your account and add posts.
