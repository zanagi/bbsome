Video sharing websites

Youtube is not used in China, instead there are several video-sharing websites in China. The two biggest are Youku and Sohu. The ones with the best video quality are iQiYi and QQ

The video-sharing websites by themselves are not suitable marketing channels but the platforms should be used when you want to post videos on Weibo or WeChat. Some Finnish companies post their videos on all these platforms but choosing one will also be fine. As in Chinese social media, it is also important to answer to customer comments on the videos.

Creating accounts on all these video-sharing websites is quite straightforward.
