Weibo tutorial

Weibo is one of the most popular way to advertise your company. It is also the most easiest one and if you are going to advertise your company Weibo is the way to start. It can be used similarly as Twitter with some own unique features.

However there are some constraints in marketing in Weibo.

  • You should make your posts in Chinese. You could make your posts in English, but this is not recomended
  • You should have chinese telephone number to register.

How to register a corporate Weibo account?

Steps:

  1. Go to http://e.weibo.com/register/register, if the link doesn't work Google Weibo registeration.
  2. Fill in the fields. Everything should be trivial. Nickname should be your Company's name or similar.
  3. After you have filled fields and pressed Sign Up now. Mobile verification pops up. You will need Chinese mobile number to do this. Also you need to choose the right area code to receive verification.
  4. You should receive activation code to your telephone and fill it in the field and press enter.
  5. After this pops up a window which wants your Nickname, Birthday Gender and Location. Everything else should be trivial except gender if you don't know Chinese. The left one is male and the right one is female. Location should be set to other.
  6. Next you should choose your interests. At least one is needed.
  7. After this you should go to your email, which you gave in the step 1. Find the verification email and click the validation link. Then your registration is done and you can start to customize your account and add posts.
